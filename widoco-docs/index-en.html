Abstract

An ontology for descibing population health dataset, created during the Older Adult Health and Wellbeing Profile project.

Introduction back to ToC

This ontology was developed to standardise how population health datasets are described. Its purpose is to make datasets easier to discover, compare, and reuse in support of population health analysis and planning. The ontology builds on established standards such as DCAT, Dublin Core, and DPV, while introducing additional terms specific to population health. It captures the core elements of a dataset and its supporting metadata, enabling consistent representation across different sources.
